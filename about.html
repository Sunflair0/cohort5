This STEM Code Camp for young students was sponsored by The AIM Institute.

Cohort five with this curriculum had seven young participants. The camp is part of a pilot program with Code.Next as the curriculum. It consists of HTML and CSS instruction and culminates into the students building their own websites.

AIM Institute is a non profit organization directed to assist low opportunity groups in Omaha, Nebraska to access the technology sector through education, training and networking.
