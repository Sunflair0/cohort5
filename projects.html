These are the final projects of the students in cohort five. Their ages ranged from 13 to 18. About half of the students had previous coding exposure with Block coding and they had an easier time learning the new languages.

One student remarked how she thought coding would be harder and that she would have to know a lot of math to be good at it, but she now understood it is just a bunch of detailed work. Most all of the students expressed they intended to keep coding in their future in some respect.
